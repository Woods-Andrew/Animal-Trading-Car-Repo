Tiger

Tigers may consume up to 40 kg (88 lbs.) of
meat at one time.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 8.2-13 feet
  • Average lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Rain forests, Grasslands, Savannas and even Mangrove swamps.

Tigers are large cats with a coat of dusty orange, with striking black stripes. They can grow to be 8.2-13 feet, and can live up to 10-15 years. They are hardly seen despite their size, since they stalk around the forest quietly. Like lions, they are an endangered species of big cats because humans are destryoing the forests that they live in.
